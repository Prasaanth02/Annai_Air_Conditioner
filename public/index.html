About Us

Annai Air Conditioner

Annai Air Conditioner is a trusted provider of air conditioning installation, servicing, and maintenance solutions for homes and businesses. We focus on delivering reliable cooling, professional workmanship, and long-lasting performance tailored to customer needs.

With experienced technicians and a commitment to quality, we handle everything from new AC installations and gas refilling to deep cleaning, repair, and annual maintenance contracts. Every service is carried out using standard tools, safety guidelines, and industry best practices.

Our goal is simple: to ensure comfort, energy efficiency, and peace of mind for our customers through dependable and transparent service.
